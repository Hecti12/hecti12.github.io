# hecti12.github.io

Personal GitHub Pages portfolio for Hector Enriquez Orellana.

The site presents IT support, help desk, systems administration, business analytics, and AI & automation projects in one static portfolio hosted at:

https://hecti12.github.io

## Structure

- `index.html` - portfolio content and sections
- `styles.css` - responsive visual design
- `app.js` - mobile navigation, active section state, and intro typing effect
- `assets/headshot.png` - professional headshot used by the homepage and sidebar
- `assets/project-*.png` - project images used by the IT and analytics cards
- `assets/hector-enriquez-orellana-resume-it.pdf` - downloadable resume used by site buttons
- `assets/ai-tools-modern-job-market.pdf` - research paper linked from the AI & Automation section
- `assets/google-admin-mfa-hardening.png` - MFA hardening reference banner used by the Google Workspace admin IT card
- `assets/ai-side-quest-pipeline.png`, `assets/ai-lumetric-films.png`, `assets/ai-augmented-analytics.png`, `assets/ai-agentic-research.png`, `assets/ai-research-paper.png` - banners for the AI & Automation cards

## AI & Automation Work

The `#ai-work` section is led by the web studio venture and shows how AI is used as a real working tool:

- **Side Quest Web Studio** (work in progress) - a web design venture with business partner Santiago, combining MBA backgrounds and IT work experience to build professional AI-built websites and deploy them on Vercel for clients across film, e-commerce, manufacturing, and the medical sector.
  - Live: https://sidequest-weblabs.vercel.app/en#work
  - First build, **Lumetric Films**: https://lumetric-films.vercel.app (repo: https://github.com/lumetricfilms/lumetric-films), a cinematic portfolio built with Claude (React, Vite, Tailwind CSS) and deployed on Vercel.
- **AI-augmented business analytics** - validating and strengthening the analytics projects with Claude Opus, ChatGPT Codex, and Google Gemini (image creation).
- **Agentic research and Excel workflows** - using AI agents for background research and to update and maintain data in Excel.
- **Research paper** - "AI Tools & Demos in the Modern Job Market (2023-2025)," linked as a PDF from the site.
